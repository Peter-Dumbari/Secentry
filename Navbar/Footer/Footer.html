About

It’s important to know who’s in your facilities, when they’re there, and for what purpose. However, it’s can be difficult keeping track of all the movements. Secentry helps you take care of your entry and exit process, allowing you to focus on other important things. Secentry also doubles as visitor’s screening tool that can simplify contact tracing, thanks to easy access to accurate visitor records.
